Unus Annus Musicae is a Ten Two and Fur Con side project,
heavily inspired by Unus Annus, the YouTube project done
by Markiplier and Ethan Nestor.

In our custom VRChat world MOMENTO MORI,
we will be hosting music events for the community to enjoy.

Unus Annus Musicae will live starting January 1st, 2026
and die January 1st, 2027 at midnight on the end of the last event.
Live, everyone will be there to watch the timer count down.
Every Friday will be an event with 4 sets, each being timestamped
by when on the death clock they are.

We will have 208 unique DJ’s only getting one opportunity to play.

When Unus Annus Musicae dies, all that will be left is an archive of all of the sets.

No pictures, no videos, no discord, no group…
nothing but the music and the memories.

We are sponsoring a campaign for The Nature Conservancy, who prioritizes in tackling climate change, protecting the ocean, land & fresh water, and providing food and water through food system solutions.
Our goal is to raise $12,000 for the global environment by the end of this project!
